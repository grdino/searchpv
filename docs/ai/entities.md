# SearchPV Entities

## Listing

A listing is a marketing record identified primarily by MLS listing number.

A property may have multiple listings over time.

## Property

A physical real estate asset. SearchPV attempts to connect multiple listing
records to one property using a property fingerprint.

## Inventory Snapshot

A dated observation of listing status and listing details.

## Closed Sale

A completed MLS transaction with a sold date and sold price.

## Zone

The highest SearchPV/MLS geography level.

## Area

A subdivision of a zone.

## Community

A subdivision of an area.

## Development

A named condominium, subdivision, building, or project.

## Colonia

A commonly used Mexican neighborhood designation. A colonia is not always
identical to an MLS community.

One colonia may:

- Correspond to one MLS community.
- Cover part of an MLS community.
- Cross multiple MLS communities.
- Be used inconsistently by listing agents and the public.

## Informal Market Area

A user-recognized location that may not be an official MLS or municipal
geography.

Examples include:

- Romantic Zone
- Zona Romántica
- Old Town
- South Shore
- Marina
- Nuevo Vallarta
