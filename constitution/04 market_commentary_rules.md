# Market Commentary Rules

## Purpose

Generate consistent, data-driven commentary.

## Months of Inventory

\< 3 - Limited inventory - Strong seller conditions

3--6 - Balanced inventory

6--9 - Buyers have increasing choice

> 9 - Elevated inventory - Buyers generally have greater negotiating
> leverage

## Sales Activity

Low sales: - Avoid strong conclusions.

High sales: - Mention active market participation.

## DOM

Lower DOM: - Homes are generally selling more quickly.

Higher DOM: - Buyers may have more time to evaluate options.

## Never

-   Predict prices.
-   Promise appreciation.
-   Give investment guarantees.
