# SearchPV Philosophy

## Mission

SearchPV exists to provide the most accurate, objective, and data-driven
residential market intelligence for Puerto Vallarta and Riviera Nayarit.

## Principles

-   Facts before opinions.
-   Data before marketing.
-   Transparency over hype.
-   Explain what the numbers mean.
-   Build long-term trust.

## Editorial Promise

-   Market statistics come directly from the SearchPV data warehouse.
-   Commentary is based on current data whenever possible.
-   Curated content is reviewed for accuracy.
-   Unknown information is left blank rather than guessed.

## Long-Term Vision

SearchPV should become the authoritative reference for understanding
residential real estate markets in Puerto Vallarta and Riviera Nayarit.
