# SearchPV Editorial Guide

## Voice

Professional, objective, locally informed, concise.

## Avoid

-   Amazing
-   Incredible
-   Luxury (unless part of the official name)
-   Best
-   Perfect investment
-   Guaranteed appreciation

## Prefer

-   Contemporary
-   Established
-   Walkable
-   Generally
-   Typically
-   Based on current MLS data

## Writing Rules

1.  Explain why something matters.
2.  Distinguish facts from commentary.
3.  Never invent facts.
4.  Keep paragraphs short.
5.  Write for buyers, sellers, and investors equally.

## Standard Sections

-   Overview
-   Building Facts
-   Amenities
-   Location
-   Lifestyle
-   Buyer Notes
-   Investor Notes
-   SearchPV Insights (dynamic)
